The Legacy Experience

Ready to train like a real fighter? At Legacy Boxing and Combat Sports Academy you'll build authentic boxing and mixed martial arts skills, boost your fitness, and develop the discipline that drives lasting results inside and outside the gym. We offer structured boxing classes and MMA training consisting of kickboxing, Muay Thai, Judo, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and more designed for beginners to advanced fighters alike.

Our Boxing Classes teach the same techniques used by Golden Gloves competitors, All-Americans, and national champions. Sparring is never required and only introduced when you're fully prepared, with safety and readiness as the top priority. You'll build real technique, conditioning, and fight IQ through focused drills like Shoulder Tag, a Cuban boxing drill that safely builds speed, coordination, and sharp defensive awareness in a fun, engaging way. Our program is ideal whether you're training for amateur boxing, preparing for your first fight, or simply looking for a real boxing gym instead of a cardio-focused class.

Our Youth Boxing Classes, open to ages 8 to 16, offer a safe and structured boxing class for kids where they build confidence, coordination, and lifelong skills. With monthly memberships and convenient drop-in options, families can stay consistent without needing to commit to seasonal camps. All instruction is led by USA Boxing certified coaches who prioritize safety, motivation, and age-appropriate training.

Memberships start at $75 per month for adult boxing or MMA, and $60 for children. Private boxing lessons, facility rentals, and drop-in options are also available. Whether you're looking to compete or just get in shape, our gym is where your boxing journey begins.

Meet Coach Roth

Coach Derek Roth is the Head Coach of the Penn State University Boxing Club Team, the top civilian boxing program in the United States since 2019. In 2023, he was named National Collegiate Boxing Association (NCBA) Coach of the Year. He brings this same level of commitment and expertise to Legacy Boxing and Combat Sports Academy.

His background includes:

  • 30 years of martial arts experience
  • 25 years of boxing experience
  • Training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu since 2009
  • Ongoing study of Kosen Judo
  • Competition experience in MMA, Kickboxing, and Muay Thai
  • Former Pennsylvania Golden Gloves boxer

Coach Roth also holds certifications in:

  • Submission Grappling Instruction
  • Kickboxing and Muay Thai Instruction
  • Personal Training
  • Nutritional Coaching

Coach Roth designs and leads all Academy programs based on years of coaching national champions. Through classes, private sessions, and continued mentorship, he helps students of all levels achieve their goals in fitness, competition, and personal development.

"The National Collegiate Boxing Association (NCBA) was formed in 1976 and is a group member of USA Boxing. The NCBA philosophy and rules provide a safe outlet for student boxers with little or no experience in the sport. The organization emphasizes the teaching of fundamental novice boxing skills while providing a safe, positive, and educational experience for student athletes. The NCBA encourages participation at the recreational, instructional, intramural, and competitive amateur boxing levels."

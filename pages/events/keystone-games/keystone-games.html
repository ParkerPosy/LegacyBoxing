About the Combat Sports Showcase

The State Games of America is the nation's largest multi-sport event for amateur athletes, bringing together competitors from all 50 states. In 2026 it comes to State College, PA – right here in Happy Valley – and the Combat Sports Showcase will be held at Legacy Boxing & Combat Sports Academy (2820 East College Ave, Suite F).

Legacy Boxing & Combat Sports Academy has been selected to host the Combat Sports Showcase, featuring both boxing and MMA competition. Athletes from local boxing clubs and collegiate programs affiliated with the National Collegiate Boxing Association (NCBA) will step into the ring to compete at the highest amateur level.

All boxing bouts are sanctioned by USA Boxing and the NCBA, ensuring top-tier competition standards, certified officials, and athlete safety protocols. MMA competition details will be announced as they become available.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the State Games of America?

The State Games of America is the nation's largest multi-sport Olympic-style event for amateur athletes. In 2026 it will be hosted in State College, PA, featuring 50+ sports and thousands of competitors from across the country.

Who can compete in the Combat Sports Showcase?

Boxing is open to amateur athletes from USA Boxing registered clubs and NCBA collegiate programs. MMA eligibility details will be announced once the event page is published. Check the Keystone Games eligibility guidelines for general requirements.

How do I register?

Boxing registration is open now through RaceReach. Click the "Register for Boxing" button on this page to go directly to the registration portal. MMA registration details are coming soon – contact us for updates.

Do I need to be a Legacy Boxing member to compete?

No. The Keystone Games Combat Sports Showcase is open to eligible amateur athletes from any registered club or collegiate program. Legacy Boxing is hosting the event, but competitors come from clubs and programs across the country.

Where can I find accommodations?

The State Games of America has partnered with the Happy Valley Adventure Bureau to provide discounted hotel rates across the State College area. Visit the official SGA 2026 accommodations page for booking options and group rates.

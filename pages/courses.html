Our Programs

Boxing

At Legacy, we teach the same techniques used by Golden Gloves competitors, All-Americans, and National Champions. Whether you're looking to compete or simply want to train like a boxer, you'll develop authentic skills, improve your conditioning, and experience the benefits of disciplined training.

Sparring is never a requirement and will only be introduced when the coach feels you are fully prepared. Our philosophy is simple: train like you're going to fight, even if you never step into the ring. This approach ensures that every participant gains the fitness, skills, and confidence of a real boxer.

Youth Boxing (Ages 8-16)

Our Youth Program is designed to teach young athletes the fundamentals of boxing and combat sports, while fostering discipline, confidence, and resilience. Under the guidance of the head coach of Penn State University's boxing team, a team with a proud 102-year legacy that currently ranks among the top 5 boxing programs in the nation, students receive high-level instruction in a supportive and structured environment.

Boxing is more than a sport; it prepares students to handle real-world challenges by setting achievable goals, building mental toughness, and teaching healthy ways to cope with stress. Training is often supported by accomplished student-athletes who serve as positive role models, enriching the experience both in and out of the ring.

Most importantly, our Youth Program is designed to be engaging and enjoyable. Judging by the enthusiasm and feedback from our young athletes, we are succeeding in making every session a rewarding experience.

Cardio Kickboxing / Boxing Circuit

Our Cardio focused classes are high-energy group workouts that blend boxing techniques with dynamic cardio exercises. Suitable for all fitness levels, these classes help you build stamina, improve coordination, enhance flexibility, and burn calories while developing lean muscle.

Key Differences:

  • Boxing focuses on authentic combat skills, emphasizing punching techniques, footwork, and defensive maneuvers.
  • Cardio Kickboxing / Boxing Circuit are fitness-driven workouts that incorporates punches, kicks, and sometimes elbows prioritizing cardiovascular health over combat proficiency.

Whether you're looking to learn boxing fundamentals or get in an intense, calorie-burning workout, we have a program to match your goals.

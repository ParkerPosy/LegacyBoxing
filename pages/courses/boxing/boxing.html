What to Expect in Our Boxing Classes

At Legacy Boxing & Combat Sports Academy, we teach the same boxing techniques used by Golden Gloves competitors, All-Americans, and National Champions. Our boxing classes are structured, progressive, and designed for adults and teens of all experience levels.

Led by USA Boxing certified coaches, our training emphasizes proper form, footwork, defense, and conditioning. We offer a disciplined, skill-focused environment where every athlete builds real boxing ability and fitness with purpose.

Sparring is never required and only introduced when you're fully prepared. Before that, we use drills like Shoulder Tag – a Cuban boxing drill that sharpens reflexes and defensive awareness in a safe and engaging way.

Whether you're training for competition, looking to get in shape, or trying boxing for the first time, Legacy offers a focused path to progress. Every session builds on fundamentals and challenges you to improve.

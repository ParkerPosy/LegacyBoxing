What Makes Legacy MMA Different?

Legacy's MMA program blends striking, takedowns, and ground control into a structured, skill-focused system. Drawing from boxing, Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), wrestling, and Judo, our curriculum teaches you how to fight in every range – with coaching that meets you at your level.

We don't just teach isolated moves. Our integrated drills turn striking and grappling into a single system you can actually use – whether you're sparring, competing, or just training to feel strong and confident.

We don't just teach techniques in isolation. Our integrated approach ensures that what you learn in drilling translates directly to controlled, live practice and functional application.

In Every MMA Class You'll:

  • Train striking fundamentals (hands, kicks, knees, elbows)
  • Develop grappling and ground control
  • Practice clinch work, takedowns, and transitions
  • Combine striking and grappling in live drills
  • Build fight-ready endurance, strength, and reaction time
